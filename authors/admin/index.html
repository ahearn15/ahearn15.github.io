Adam Hearn

Graduate Student, Data Science for Public Policy

Georgetown University

Originally from Athens, GA, Adam Hearn is a current graduate student studying Data Science for Public Policy at the McCourt School of Public Policy at Georgetown University. As a graduate of Rhodes College (2019), Adam acquired his B.A. in Economics and minor in Computer Science. Adam enjoys studying the intersection of these two disciplines, by using data mining and collection to apply econometric models and consequently advance and implement public policy. Adam’s areas of interest include econometric theory, data science, machine learning, and higher education policy.
